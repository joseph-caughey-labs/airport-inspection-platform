## What this is

The Airport Inspection Platform is a production-style demonstration of an AI-assisted airport inspection system. It simulates airport operational workflows (FOD detection, runway inspection, snowbank compliance, etc.) but is **not** a deployed product, certified inspection system, or substitute for any real airport ops platform.
